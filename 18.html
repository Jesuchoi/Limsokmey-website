YOUTH VOLUNTEERS

When I study at Hunsen balang High school in grade 10 to grade 12. I am a youth volunteers of Cambodia Red Cross. Starting work for the first time, I learned about firefighting from experts to help people in trouble.
After that, I went to inspect the houses of the villagers and launched a campaign to apply Abet to kill worms, eradicate tiger mosquitoes to reduce and eradicate dengue fever.
Sometimes we run a campaign to clean up the city by picking up trash on the streets.
And we did a lot of volunteer work and learned a lot from this job.
