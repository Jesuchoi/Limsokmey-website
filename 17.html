MY DREAM COUNTRY

Switzerland

Switzerland is a dream country that I want to visit. Switzerland is a small country in Europe that attracts many tourists each year due to its beautiful landscapes, including lakes, mountains, villages, and ski resorts. The author dreams of taking their family to Switzerland one day to experience attractions like Mount Pilatus, a popular hiking destination accessible via the world's steepest cogwheel train or gondolas. With its natural beauty, modern cities, and variety of activities, Switzerland remains a top tourist destination for people worldwide.
